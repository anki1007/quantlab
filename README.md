# QuantLab

### Institutional Equity Leadership Intelligence Platform

QuantLab is an AI-powered equity research and market intelligence platform designed to identify institutional leadership before it becomes obvious to the broader market.

The platform combines Stage Analysis, Relative Strength, Sector Rotation, Institutional Activity, Earnings Momentum, and Market Breadth into a unified framework for discovering high-quality equity opportunities.

Unlike traditional stock screeners that rely on simple filters, QuantLab continuously evaluates market structure, leadership rotation, accumulation patterns, and momentum characteristics to rank equities based on their probability of becoming future market leaders.

---

## Core Philosophy

The strongest performing equities typically exhibit a combination of:

* Strong Relative Strength
* Rising Institutional Participation
* Sector Leadership
* Industry Leadership
* Earnings Acceleration
* High-Quality Base Structures
* Breakout Confirmation

QuantLab focuses on identifying these characteristics early and ranking opportunities based on objective data rather than subjective opinions.

## Leadership Framework

QuantLab evaluates equities using a multi-factor scoring model:

```text
Leadership Score

├── Relative Strength
├── Relative Strength Acceleration
├── Trend Quality
├── Institutional Activity
├── Sector Strength
├── Industry Strength
├── Earnings Momentum
├── Market Breadth
└── Breakout Quality
```

The result is a ranked list of equities with the strongest institutional leadership characteristics.
